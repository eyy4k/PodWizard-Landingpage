Plans, billing, and cancellation

Free plan (Starter)

The Starter plan is free forever and includes up to 3 episode generations per month. We reserve the right to adjust what is included in the free plan with 30 days' notice.

Paid plan (Creator)

The Creator plan is billed monthly at $29/mo or annually at $19/mo ($228/yr). A 14-day free trial is included — no credit card is required to start. At the end of the trial, you will be prompted to enter payment details to continue.

Billing

To cancel: Settings → Billing → Cancel subscription. Your account reverts to the free Starter plan at the end of your paid period.
