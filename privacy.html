01

Who we are

PodWizard is an AI-powered podcast production platform that helps creators research, script, and grow their shows. Our service is accessible at podpulse-creator-flow.base44.app.

02

Information we collect

Information you give us directly

  • Account information: your email address, display name, and password when you sign up.
  • Show settings: your podcast name, niche, RSS feed URL, and show artwork.
  • Content you create: episode topics, scripts, notes, guest information, and sponsorship details.
  • Payment information: processed by Stripe. We never see or store your full card details.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: which features you use, how often you generate episodes, and how you navigate the app.
  • Device and browser info: browser type, OS, IP address, and general location (country/region).
  • Performance data: errors and crash reports used to improve reliability.
03

How we use your information

  • To provide the service: generating your episodes, running the AI agents, storing your history.
  • To improve and personalise: your ratings and feedback personalise future generations for your show.
  • To send relevant emails: account confirmations, billing receipts, product updates. Unsubscribe from marketing emails any time.
  • To prevent abuse and meet legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your content to train AI models for other companies. Your episodes are yours.

04

How we share your information

  • Service providers: cloud hosting, Stripe (payments), email delivery, and AI inference APIs — all bound by data processing agreements.
  • Platform integrations: when you publish to Spotify, Apple Podcasts, or YouTube, your episode data is shared with those platforms.
  • Team members: members you invite can see your show's content based on their assigned roles.
  • Legal requirements: if required by law or court order, we will disclose and notify you where permitted.
05

AI-generated content and your data

  • Your episode topics are sent to AI APIs (such as Claude by Anthropic) to generate content. This is not retained by those APIs for training beyond their own policies.
  • Your star ratings are stored by PodWizard and used to personalise future generations for your show specifically.
  • We do not use your episode content to train our own or third-party AI models.

Your creative work belongs to you. Content you generate with PodWizard is yours to publish and own — we make no claim over it.

06

Data retention

  • Account data: kept until you delete your account.
  • Episode content and history: kept until you delete them or your account.
  • Payment records: kept for 7 years (financial regulations).
  • Usage logs: kept for 90 days.

When you delete your account, we permanently delete your personal data within 30 days.

07

Your rights

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: ask us to fix inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: ask us to delete your data.
  • Portability: receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: object to certain uses including direct marketing.

Email privacy@podwizard.io to exercise any of these rights. We respond within 30 days.

08

Cookies and tracking

  • Essential cookies: session tokens that keep you logged in. Cannot be opted out of.
  • Analytics cookies: anonymous usage analytics. Do not identify you personally.
  • Preference cookies: remember your settings, such as dark/light mode.

We do not use advertising cookies or sell tracking data to ad networks.

09

Data security

  • All data encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Passwords hashed and never stored in plain text.
  • Payments handled entirely by Stripe (PCI DSS Level 1 certified).
  • Production system access limited to authorised personnel only.

If you believe your account is compromised, contact security@podwizard.io immediately.

10

Children's privacy

PodWizard is not intended for anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Contact us to have a child's account deleted promptly.

11

Changes to this policy

When we make material changes, we will notify you by email and in-app at least 14 days before they take effect. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
