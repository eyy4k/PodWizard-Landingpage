The backstory

Why we built this.

PodWizard started with a simple frustration: why does making a podcast take so long? You have a topic. You have a voice. You have something worth saying. But before your audience hears it, there's a mountain of unglamorous work between you and publish.

Research takes hours. Scripts take hours. Thumbnails. SEO. Social clips. Chasing guests. Pitching sponsors. By the time most podcasters finish one episode, they've already lost momentum for the next.

We believed AI could change this — but only if built specifically for podcasters, with the right quality bars, niche awareness, and pipeline. So we built PodWizard v6.

The 9-step pipeline doesn't just generate stuff. It researches like a producer, scripts like a writer, designs like a creative director, optimises like an SEO specialist, and double-checks everything like a QA engineer — automatically, every time.
